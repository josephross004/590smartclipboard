High-Low Down Screen Action

Advanced Coaching Report
Coach's Analysis

The play emphasizes synchronization between the screener and the cutter to exploit a man-to-man defense. By establishing the center's screen early, the point guard can effectively navigate around the pick to find space in the corner.
Step-by-Step Breakdown

    1Center moves from the high post to set a stationary down screen at the wing.
    2Point guard waits for the screen to be fully set before curling around the high side.
    3Shooting guard delivers a skip pass over the defense to the point guard in the opposite corner.
