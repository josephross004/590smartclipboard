Zone Perimeter Overload Pin-Down

Advanced Coaching Report
Coach's Analysis

This play effectively exploits the gaps in the 2-3 zone by establishing multiple off-ball screens simultaneously. The timing of the PG's cut is critical, as it relies on the SF screener being set before the ball reaches the perimeter target.
Step-by-Step Breakdown

    1SF, PF, and C move to set firm screens to pin the zone defenders in the paint and on the wings.
    2The PG cuts through the middle off the SF's screen while the ball is passed from the backcourt to the SG at the top of the key.
